## Copy Principles (apply throughout)

- Lead with the business problem, not the technology
- "Built for your business" / "fits exactly" as recurring language
- Replace generic "AI systems" references with the triad: **AI agents, custom software, automated workflows**
- The "not one size fits all" argument is made once (section 3) — don't restate it in later sections
